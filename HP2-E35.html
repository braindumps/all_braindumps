Ethernet Is Now Enhanced – Ethernet and the storage industry have been marching
toward a set of standards that will converge classic IP and storage networking
infrastructure. This is exactly what the HP integrated FlexFabric Adapter does in the new
HP ProLiant G7 server blades.
Converged 10Gb Ethernet (10GbE) LOMs – Until HP’s integrated FlexFabric Adapter

HP’s FlexFabric to create up to eight virtual Network Interface Cards (vNICs) across the
dual 10GbE ports to lower costs of connectivity, reduce cabling complexity, save up to 66%
on connectivity power and unify management under HP’s BladeSystem software.
