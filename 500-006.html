What will happen if the local zone on a Cisco TelePresence VCS does not have a search
rule?
A. Calls to locally registered devices will fail.
B. Calls from locally registered devices will fail.
C. Calls to externally registered devices will fail.
D. Calls from externally registered devices will fail.
Answer: A
Explanation:
If the alias is found within the Local Zone, in one of the external zones, or a routing
destination is returned by the policy service, the VCS attempts to place the call.
If the alias is not found, it responds with a message to say that the call has failed.
